TextBook

The solution to teacher-parent communication

No more having parent cell numbers in your personal phone. TextBook allows you to keep all of those numbers in a separate, secure place and text parents in real time.

Organize

Keep track of every message

Keep all of your parent contacts tied to a student and receive alerts when you get a new message.

Be Productive

Use text blasts and set homework

TextBook allows you to send group text blasts to all your students' parents and allows you to set homework for each class. Parents can text 'homework' to your TextBook number and receive an automated message with the homework you set.

Advanced Tech

Powered by Twilio

TextBook uses Twilio, a platform to make phone calls and send SMS messages via the Internet.
